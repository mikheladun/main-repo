Parlance: A Service-Oriented Framework

Our technology teams are constantly seek new and dynamic ways to improve the delivery experience. We created a development tool, a proprietary XML-based framework called Parlance. Parlance, in a nutshell, is a conceptual framework for developing scalable software solutions through the use of reusable software components and enterprise services. The service-oriented architecture (SOA) framework is built on proven open source technologies and industry standards and intended to support other frameworks and components. Originally developed for research, Parlance now speeds delivery of our custom-software projects and enables our clients to rapidly build new systems or rollout updates to existing ones.
