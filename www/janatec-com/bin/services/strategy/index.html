Internet Strategy

The Internet will continue to be embraced by organizations as a mainstream environment when providing information and services, and dealing with customers. Developing an effective Internet strategy demands much more than building a website. An internet strategy is long term planning executed to make a web site an integral part of a company's long term success. It is an exciting opportunity to incorporate the Internet into the total business strategy.

Our internet strategy services focus on four core areas:

  1. Visibility: We use a combination of search engine optimization, pay-per-click advertising, affiliate marketing, and PR campaigns to help clients improve their visibility online.
  2. Impression: On the Web, the experience is the brand. We help clients create a great impression by designing a visually appealing, functional, unique and easy-to-use web site.
  3. Conversion: We help client develop strategies and ways to convert each user into a customer once they establish an impression and their curiosity is triggered.
  4. Retention: We help clients create "sticky" features that motivate users to re-visit their web site again and again. These elements not only increase the conversion rate but also generate means of increased business from existing clients.
