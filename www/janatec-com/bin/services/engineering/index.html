Product Engineering

We offer product development and engineering services both as an extension of your own engineering team and also via an outsourced delivery model. These services accelerate the creation of software solutions to increase your competitiveness and extend the principles of standard application development methods to meet specific requirements of enterprise software products.

Our product engineering team helps you define and implement innovative product features and functionality to satisfy evolving market demands. We also enable you to launch compelling products ahead of your competition while reducing marketing and technology risks.

Our product engineering expertise ensures that the software products we deliver are created with additional attention to quality, robustness and flexibility of architecture, to enable their successful deployment by a wide range of clients across different geographic areas, infrastructures and often business sectors.

We deliver high grade software products that are:

  1. Portable: To satisfy multi-installation at many sites and run on multiple platforms, with permutations of browsers, web-servers, application servers, databases, and operations systems.
  2. Scalable: To satisfy multiple platforms and different classes of user.
  3. Usable: Intuitive and easy to use, with minimal training.
  4. Reliable: Reliable and easy to support by people with limited knowledge of its functionality.
  5. Installable: Easy to install and upgrade.
  6. Hostable: Software Product Engineering for an extranet requires special skills, especially where several companies share a database. Resolving issues of security, network performance, remote support, diagnostics and data modelling is critical.
  7. Translatable: To avoid language issues affecting performance.
  8. Configurable: The emphasis is always on configuration over customization.
  9. Securable: Designed to confine access to authorized users on authorized hardware for a limited time.
  10. Extensible: To interface with external systems without extensive customization.
  11. Versionable: Easy to version, support, track and align.
